GEORG EVEREST PEAK, MUSSOORIE

One of the popular tourist destinations in Dehradunis Sir George Everest House at Mussoorie. It is perched on a rock overlooking the lovely Doon Valley. Everest House, also referred to as "The Park Estate," served as Sir George Everest's residence, observatory, and laboratory while he was Surveyor General of India

It provides breathtaking views of the snow-covered Himalayan peaks as well as expansive vistas of the Doon Valley. The Park Estate, commonly known as Sir George Everest’s Home and Laboratory, was constructed in 1832. These days the George Everest House and George Everest Peak are famous for camping, picnics, and adventure activities. It is becoming more well-known every day due to its proximity to Dehradun and Mussoorie.
